## FAQ: Everything You Need to Know About Personalized Gifts for the Person Who Has Everything

**Q: How long does customization take?**  
Most custom photo shirts take about 3-5 business days to produce, plus shipping time. If you're ordering for a specific date, plan at least two weeks ahead to be safe.

**Q: What photo quality do I need?**  
Higher resolution is always better, but most companies can work with standard smartphone photos. Just avoid heavily filtered or pixelated images for the best results.

**Q: Can I return personalized items?**  
Typically no, since they're custom-made. Double-check your spelling, photo selection, and sizing before you finalize the order.

**Q: Are these shirts true to size?**  
Generally yes, but check the specific sizing charts for each product. Most run unisex sizing, which means they're slightly roomier than women's fitted styles.

**Q: What if I'm not creative enough to design something?**  
The beauty of these services is that they do the design work for you. You just upload the photo, and they apply professional vintage effects, graphics, and layouts.

**Q: How do I care for custom photo shirts?**  
Wash inside out in cold water and hang dry or tumble dry low. This helps preserve the print quality for years. Avoid ironing directly on the design.
