### FAQ

**Do I need professional photos for these?**
No! Honestly, iPhone photos work best. The grainier and more candid, the more authentic the vintage look feels. High-res is good, but perfection isn't required.

**How do I wash these custom shirts?**
Since they have large printed graphics, I always recommend washing them inside out on cold and hanging them to dry. It keeps the print from cracking too early.

**Are these unisex?**
Yeah, most of these come in standard unisex sizing, which adds to that oversized, comfy retro fit.
